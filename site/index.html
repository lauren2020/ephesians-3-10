The religious leaders of “God’s chosen people” as well as Satan and his demons were denied knowledge about God’s Purpose-For-Creation because “none of the princes of this world knew: for had they known it, they would not have crucified the Lord of glory” (1Corinthians 2:8). The “crucifixion of the Lord of glory” had always been God’s Purpose-For-Creation “from before the foundation of the world.”

The reality is that no one who claims to be “born again of the Spirit” would ever vote to “crucify the Lord of glory.” And yet the crucifixion of “the Lord of glory” is exactly what God intended when He created everything consistent with His Ephesians 3:10 Purpose Statement. That’s the reason WHY an omniscient God had to “hide the mystery” from everyone until after Jesus had been crucified and until after “the church” had been established by the Apostle Paul. Otherwise, “the Lord of glory” would have never been crucified simply because every God believer would have voted against it.

You and I and every other human who is “born again of the Spirit” (John 3:6) would have sabotaged God’s Purpose-For-Creation by voting against the crucifixion of “the Lord of glory.” Please meditate on that painful reality as you continue reading.
